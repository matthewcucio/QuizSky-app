How to Use QuizSky

Steps to Get Started:

  1. Enter your name on the homepage and click Start Quiz.
  2. Select a subject of your choice from the available options.
  3. Answer the 5 multiple-choice questions in the quiz.
  4. Submit your answers to see your score and improve your knowledge.
  5. Choose whether to take a quiz again or go back to home.

Need help? Contact us at support@quizsky.com.
